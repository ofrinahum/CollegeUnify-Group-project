About Us

Welcome to CollegeUnify, a task management application designed with your success—and balance—in mind! Created by a group of college students from Towson University, our mission is to empower fellow students to take control of their schedules while ensuring that they can still make time for themselves. With our smart scheduling system, we prioritize not just your goals but also your well-deserved free time, because we believe break time is just as important as go time. As students ourselves, we intimately understand the challenges of balancing classes, assignments, and personal commitments. That’s why we’ve built CollegeUnify to be more than just a planner—it’s a tool designed with empathy for your unique journey. Join us as we redefine productivity and make scheduling a tool for success, not stress!
